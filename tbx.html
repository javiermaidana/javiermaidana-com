What is TBX

A business platform for the industry

TBX Latam is a platform built to support the growth and professionalization of the tattoo and body art industry in Latin America.

It connects brands, distributors and operators through strategy, market understanding and long-term vision.

Focus Areas

What TBX works on

Market Development
Identifying opportunities and building sustainable growth in LATAM.
Distribution Strategy
Developing and strengthening regional distribution networks.
Brand Positioning
Helping brands grow within the professional segment.
Regional Strategy
Long-term vision adapted to the realities of Latin America.

TBX is not about selling products — it's about building a stronger and more structured business ecosystem in the region.
